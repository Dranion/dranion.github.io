Dolphin research internship in Portugal. We mostly did photo identifaction of the migratory groups which move through the area, along with recording group's activity, size, etc. AIMM had partnered with dolphin watching boats but we also had our own boat.

In addition to the normal identification, as a group we also performed a biopsy, used a tow net to collected both zoo- and phyto-plankton for analysis, and collected biological samples of limpits, barnicles, and other organisms from the shore.
