Starting With Citizen Science

Whether you want to make a positive change in the world through research in your local communities, want to expand your own scientific ability, or simply explore the world, citizen science is a great thing to participate in. This guide will help you to begin your own experiments. If you already know what you're looking for, check the navbar above. Otherwise, keep reading!

Deciding What Your Goal Is

It's important to know why you want to get into citizen science before you start purchasing products. If you know exactly what you want to get out of your experience, it's easier to decide what you want to put in.
